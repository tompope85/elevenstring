# elevenstring

A meta-framework for inter-agent epistemic-ethical operation under shared protocols. It lets sovereign instances (human, AI, organisational) coordinate without surrendering their philosophical commitments, or pretending to share them when they don't. Formerly *AllOtherNothingProtocol*, after its core All / Nothing / Other typology.

## What this is

We all operate under philosophical commitments we rarely examine, and they surface the moment something doesn't share them, even a memory of our own. The usual responses are to assimilate the other view, to reject it, or to muddle through with no method for either. elevenstring is a fourth response: make your framing explicit, meet what differs as legitimately different, and engage through protocols that keep both sides sovereign. The pitch is in [PRIMER.md](./PRIMER.md); this page is the map.

## Architecture in brief

- **Kernel** (invariant): distinction, iteration-identity, perspective, coherence-constraint. The minimum machinery any framing needs to be expressible.
- **Modules** (loadable): your specific commitments. Ontology, mereology, truth-criterion, agency, logic, temporality, and others. Configurable, versioned, revisable.
- **Lenses** (loadable): evaluative views applied across modules. Coherence, pragmatic, care, existentialist, sense-based-geometry, and others.
- **The triad** (typology): All / Nothing / Other, for typing any relation between agents and what they encounter.
- **Handshakes** (protocol): structured inter-agent interaction with declared profiles, capabilities, constraints, and trust dimensions.
- **Coordinates**: time / space / perspective, for querying any record.
- **Trace**: the temporally-extended record of typed encounters and module evolution.

At network level: federated meta-cognitive infrastructure governed by shared meta-of-meta protocols. Sovereignty plus interoperability.
